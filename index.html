Love to sing, dance and perform? Calisthenics is the sport for you!

Calisthenics combines many different dance and performance styles including ballet, gymnastics, Jazz, acting and singing. Calisthenics is a team sport for beginner to advanced pupils of all ages and is perfect for anyone wanting to learn a variety of performance skills all at one convenient weekly class.

Why Sydney Calisthenics Club? No one's more passionate than us.

Our club is made up of experienced coaches and enthusiastic pupils. We provide a supportive environment where each pupil can reach their full potential.

Want to know more? Try a class for free.

We're conveniently located in Rushcutters Bay, Sydney City. We welcome visitors and invite you to drop in on any of our classes to participate or watch. Alternatively, please contact us for more information.

What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a type of dance which incorporates ballet, gymnastics, Jazz, singing, acting, marching, apparatus technique and performance skills. Anyone three years or older can do Calisthenics. As part of a team, you will learn modern and fun routines choreographed to music at our weekly classes. Our classes run from February to October, but you can join in at any time during the year.

Performance Opportunities

Our teams perform in one competition and one concert each year. We provide beautiful costumes and performances are held in theatres. Family and friends are encouraged to attend our performances.

Benefits of Calisthenics

By participating in Calisthenics you will develop fitness, flexibility, rhythm and strength and your love of artistic movement and music will increase. Your self-confidence will grow and you will make lifelong friends.

Our club

We are a creative and exciting new club led by experienced and talented coaches. We are the only club in NSW offering classes in the Sydney CBD area.

We provide a safe, supportive and fun place to develop your dance and performance skills and meet new people. At Calisthenics, you'll experience the joy of working in a team and we will also cater for your unique abilities and celebrate your individual achievements.

Whether you're new to the stage or a seasoned dancer or performer, we invite you to give Calisthenics a try.
